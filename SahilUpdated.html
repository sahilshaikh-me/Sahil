About Me

I’m an XR and Game Developer specializing in Augmented, Virtual, and Mixed Reality (AR/VR/MR). With a strong focus on immersive technologies, I design and build multi-user experiences that span both entertainment and enterprise applications. My work blends interactive storytelling, real-time 3D environments, and system-level development to create games, simulations, and maintenance tools that are not only functional but truly engaging. From collaborative VR worlds to AR-based training and industrial software, I aim to push the boundaries of what’s possible in spatial computing. Whether it's building from the ground up or integrating with existing systems, I’m passionate about using emerging tech to create intuitive, immersive solutions that feel natural—and unforgettable.
